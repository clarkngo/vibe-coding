One Runway

Air traffic control simulator. Manage a single runway, prioritize landings and departures, handle emergencies, and keep the airport safe.

Weather: Clear Sky

How to Play

  1. Select an aircraft from an incoming, departure, or holding queue.
  2. Use LAND, TAKE OFF, HOLD, or GO AROUND to control the runway.
  3. Prioritize medical, emergency, and low-fuel flights to avoid penalties.
  4. Watch weather, radar activity, and delays to keep the airport safe.
